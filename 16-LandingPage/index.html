A second brain, for you, forever.

Everything is connected

The human brain is non-linear: we jump from idea to idea, all the time. Your second brain should work the same.

In Obsidian, making and following connections is frictionless. Tend to your notes like a gardener; at the end of the day, sit back and marvel at your own knowledge graph.

Graph View

The graph is your knowledge at a glance. Explore, observe, and stay motivated. With group, filter, and display options, see things your way. Local graph shows connections around you. Get inspired while writing.

Backlinks

Converge your thoughts. Broaden your view to see this note in other contexts. Link relevant pages with one click.

Outgoing Links

Diverge your thoughts. See all existing and potential links from this note. Turn mentions into links with one click.

Stay in sync

Obsidian Sync keeps your vaults in sync on multiple devices, including in our mobile app. Immediately pick up where you left off when switching between devices.

Private & encrypted

Even when using Obsidian Sync, your data is still primarily on your own hard disk. All data going out of or into your device is encrypted. Write freely in complete privacy.

Your time machine

Obsidian Sync comes with one year of version history, making it a breeze to revert to an earlier version if anything goes wrong. All your version histories are encrypted too.
